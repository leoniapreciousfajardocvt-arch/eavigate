📚 About Emilio Aguinaldo College – Cavite Campus

🏛️ Overview: Emilio Aguinaldo College - Cavite Campus is a private, non-sectarian, co-educational higher education institution located in Burol Main, Dasmariñas City, Cavite, Philippines. Established in 1996, it serves a large and diverse student community across one of EAC's two main campuses (other in Manila).

📍 Campus Location & Contact

Website: www.eac.edu.ph

🎓 Academic Programs

  • Senior High School: ABM, STEM, HumSS; Technical-Vocational: ICT, Culinary, Tourism, Automotive, Electronics
  • Undergraduate: Business & Management, Criminology, Nursing & Health Sciences, Engineering & Technology, Psychology & Humanities, Law (Juris Doctor)
  • Graduate: Education, Criminal Justice, Nursing, related fields

🏗️ Campus Facilities

Multiple academic buildings, library, science/computer labs, covered court, sports oval, student-friendly spaces. Continuously expanding infrastructure.

👥 Student Life

Active student organizations, academic support, professional career preparation. Emphasizes character development, community involvement, internships, industry partnerships.

📝 Admissions

Apply online or visit admissions office. Welcomes high school graduates, transfers, college/graduate seekers.
